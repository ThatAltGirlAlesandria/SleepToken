Sleep Token is a British progressive metal band from London, England who recently gain world wide recognition due to their single The Summoning. The group is entirely annoymous via the use of Masks. The front man goes by the moniker of Vessel, the remaining members go by numbers 2-4, however, prefer them to be written as roman numerals II, III, IV.
